About Unifit

UniFit Admissions Pvt. Ltd., established in 2019 by Mr. Sidharth Gupta, aims to facilitate seamless international university admissions. Leveraging Mr. Gupta's global experience, UniFit Admissions boasts a dedicated team of education counselors and an efficient backend system. With a vast network in India and Nepal, we represent 95 esteemed UK academic institutions.

Established in 2029, UniFit was birthed with an intention to help millions of agents pan India. These individuals possess a burning entrepreneurial spirit but limited resources to fuel their ambitions. UniFit has helped thousands of agents to succeed in their small scale businesses through its university connections globally. We have successfully assisted a myriad of aspiring students by providing them with a clear career pathway. Thus, I am committed to making UniFit a trailblazer in the field of international education consultancy as it stand as a steadfast pillar of support for ambitious agents and students.

Our Core Values

Admissions prioritizes comprehensive and meritorious applications. Backed by 21 years of UK university partnerships, our Risk Assessment Tool ensures application integrity. We support resource-constrained students and streamline the application process with our CRM software.

Our Vision

Our vision is to foster collaboration between local businesses and international institutions, driving quality-oriented results and exceptional value for all stakeholders.
