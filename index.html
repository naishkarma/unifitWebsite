Course Finder

We are thrilled to introduce an innovative addition to your CRM platform that will transform the way you searchfor educational institutions. Our 'Course Search' feature is designed to simplify your institution search process, promissing a remarkable experience for both you and your students.

Tool grants you access to a database of over 48,671 courses globally.
It provides a user-friendly interface where you can fine-tune your search by selection key parameters such as Country, Intake, and student's year of interest.
Our Advanced Search option offers a wide range of filters, including Program Level, Country, University, Location, Intake, and Admission Requirements.
You can also discover details on course duration, study areas, and specialized disciplines.
Moreover, it allow you to assess English proficience prerequisites for each program, ensuring your students meet the necessary language standards.
Thus, explore the future of education with 'Course Search', where endless possibilities are just a few clicks away.
